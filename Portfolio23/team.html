Team Project

Webpage built as a team, with me as designer, for the department of Web & Mobile Development at our school. My vision was to create a look remanisent of the movie Tron. A futuristic, tech world.

Announcement Carousel

Built to look layered as if looking at the screen of a viewing device. Blue backlight glow effect.

Each announcement appears with sliding animation when either an arrow or circle is clicked on.

Admin Login Page

Background image in red and blue hues covers the whole screen.

Admin login form is centeral focus & transparent to reveal background image.

Newsletter

Most recent newsletter iframe with blue backlight glow effect.

Call to action linking to the school website.

School Calendar

Google calendar.

Created dark mode to match feel of page and used blue backlight glow effect.

Event Cards

Created event cards to highlight upcoming class events.

Event Cards Overlay

Animated overlay, swings in on hinge from top left corner when arrow in bottom left corner is clicked on.

Side Navigation

When hover over hamburger menu it changes to an arrow out.

Upon clicking this arrow the side navigation is extended to full view.

Side Navigation Highlighted

On hover the side navigation items highlight like neon red lights.

Video Page

Currently has placeholder videos that will be class videos from events.

Each video has a title heading and red backlight effect.

Newsletter Archive Page

Animated background as blue light orbs that rise to top of page while increasing in size and then fading out.
